Ingredients

Natural Orange Juice

 250ml

Lemon

 1/2
Sugar 16grams
Mint Leafs 3
Straberries (cut) 5
Kiwi (peeled and cut) 2
Banana (peeled and cut) 1
Vodka 200ml
Licor 100ml
White Rum 100ml
Balsam 100ml
Red Fruits Cider 330ml
Carbonated Soda 250ml
Bottle of Rosé Brut Sparkling Wine 1

Preparation

First place the pilled and cut fruit (Straberries, Kiwi, Banana) in a wide, deep jar or bowl that can take at least 3 to 4 liters. Add the Sugar. Squeeze half of a Lemon. Pour the Natural Orange Juice, the Vodka, the Licor, the White Rum, the Balsam. Stir the content of the jar and let it sit in the fridge for about 1 hour.

After taking the drink from the fridge add some Ice, the Red Fruits Cider, the Carbonated Soda and the Rosé Brut Sparkling Wine.

Garnish it with Mint Leafs, stir it very gently and it's ready to serve!
